Protein Information

CHECK THE PROTEIN INFO

Please, note that this form shows the description of the proteins involved in the "crystal" you have selected to be reused. The information contained here must not be modified.

Confirm that the set of proteins involved in the chosen complex is exactly that one you have used in your simulations. When mutant proteins appear in the form, pay special attention to the "Protein Mutations" sections corresponding to those mutants!!! Mutations in your simulation must exactly match the ones in this form for each mutated protein.

If one of the proteins in your simulation is not present in this form, please click the ‘New Submission’ button in the initial ‘Simulation Submission’ page and enter your simulations as a new system from scratch.
