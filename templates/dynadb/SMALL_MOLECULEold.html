Small Molecule Information

FILL IN THE SMALL MOLECULE FORM

Please, provide information of all non-protein molecules in your system.

Small molecule information contains data for compounds and molecules that are not proteins, including lipids, solvent and ions. General compound information can be retrieved from PubChem Compound and ChEMBL databases. User must also provide information about elemental composition, connectivity, net charge, protonation states and tautomerical form through uploading a MDL Molfile or an SDFile. Isotopical composition is optional. PubChem CIDs for typical molecules as water or most common ions will be provided. Coordinate information must be provided later on model (step 3) or dynamics forms (step 4).

Only molecule structures that have coordinates coming from experimental data or that are crucial for the setting of the simulation must be included in model by checking the field “Present in model” (e. g. crystalized-like waters or very stable ions, but not water molecules or ions with randomly generated coordinates). Otherwise chemical structures must be included into the dynamics form (step 4).

For more information on this form, see the docs.
