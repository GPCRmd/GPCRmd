Step 1: General information

Pre-submission:

Before to start a submission it is necessary to do a previous preparation:

1) Prepare the files need it for the upload:

  • Topology. Extensions: psf, top
  • Trajectory. Extensions: dcd, xtc, trj, trr
  • Simulation parameters. Extensions: prm, prmtop, tar.gz, tgz
  • Simulation protocol. Extensions: zip, tar.gz, tgz

As optional, add other files that you consider necessary for the submission:

  • Other. Extensions: tar.gz, tgz

2) Trajectories must be aligned to transmembrane part of the receptor.


Do you have any question?

Please contact us at info@gpcrmd.org and we will help you. Remember that for more information, see the docs.
