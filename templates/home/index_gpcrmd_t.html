A new database for GPCRs

GPCRmd is a database of GPCR MD with the purpose to provide storage, dissemination and analysis of this data.

It includes at least one member of each solved GPCR class, both in the apo form and bound to different ligands (natural ligands, agonists or antagonists).

GPCRmd also provides several online analysis tools, visualizaiton tools and annotatons from other databases to characterize the simulations.

Use our browser to find the GPCR that you want to study.

Users can also sumbit their simulaitons to increase the impact and citability of their work by making it available and to the community
