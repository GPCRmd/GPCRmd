GPCRmd response to COVID-19

GPCRmd, in collaboration with Exscalate4Cov, contributes to the fight against the COVID-19 pandemic by applying their expertise to develop a web platform for the dissemination of molecular dynamics simulations for SARS-CoV-2 related proteins. For that, it will provide a collection of online tools to easily visualize, analyze and share such data.
