Crystalized Components Info

CHECK THE CRYSTALIZED COMPONENTS INFO

Please, find here the information on the crystalized components you have requested to be reused in your simulation submission. Make sure the data contained here matches the one corresponding to your simulation.

Information appearing in the ‘Curated protein information’ section must be identical to the one concerning your simulation. Resname, Mol name, Num of mol and Type fields in the ‘Information of crystalized small molecules' section must match data in your simulation as well.

If a perfect match exists, please click the 'Check Protein Information' and 'Check Small Molecule Information' links at the bottom of the form to confirm matching of further system details. Otherwise, you have to submit your simulation as a new system from scratch by clicking the ‘New Submission’ button in the initial ‘Simulation Submission’ page and following the whole Submission index.
