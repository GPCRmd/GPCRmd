The online resource for GPCR simulations

GPCRmd is an online platform with web-based visualization capabilities and a comprehensive analysis toolbox that allows scientists from any discipline to visualize, inspect, and analyse GPCR molecular dynamics.

It is a community-driven initiative with the final aim to accelerate basic research and beyond the discovery of more efficient and safer therapeutics.

28 - 30 of October 2019

GPCRmd at the ERNEST GPCR COST-action Conference

GPCRmd was presented by Dr. Jana Selent at the first conference of the European Research Network on Signal Transduction (ERNEST, COST Action CA18133). Further discussion was also held at the poster session. The meeting took place at the Queen's University Belfast in Northern Ireland on October 28-30, 2019. It focused on recent advances in GPCRs pharmacology as well as systems biology strategies for advancing the development of a multidimensional holistic map of GPCR signal transduction.

23rd of October 2019

GPCRmd in the Novo Nordisk Foundation symposium "G Protein-Coupled Receptors: New Techniques, Tools and Concepts"

We discussed about GPCRmd in a flash talk followed by poster presentation at the G Protein-Coupled Receptors: New Techniques, Tools and Concepts symposium, organized by the Novo Nordisk Foundation and the Universuty of Copenhagen in Copenhagen, Denmark.

9 - 11 of October 2019

GPCRmd in the 8th GDR3545-GPCR international meeting

The GPCRmd database and its main features were exposed in several posters at the 8th GDR3545-GPCR international meeting, held in Montpellier, France, from October 9th to 11th 2019. The meeting tackled the interplay between the state-of-the-art on GPCRs, drug discoveries and innovation.
