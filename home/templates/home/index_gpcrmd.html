The new database for GPCR MD

GPCRmd is a database of GPCR MD with the purpose to provide storage, dissemination and analysis of this data.

It contains several analysis and visualisation tools to characterize the simulations.

Users can also sumbit their simulaitons make them available to the community

20th of September 2019

GPCRmd will be presented at the ERNEST GPCR COST-action Conference

The first conference of the European Research Network on Signal Transduction (ERNEST, COST Action CA18133) will take place at the Queen's University Belfast in Northern Ireland on October 28-30, 2019. It will focus on recent advances in GPCRs pharmacology as well as systems biology strategies for advancing the development of a multidimensional holistic map of GPCR signal transduction.
