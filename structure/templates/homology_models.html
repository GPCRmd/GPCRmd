Homology models - Less Model and More Crystal



Methods

  • Automated pipeline for the method that gave the best 5-HT1B RMSD in GPCR Dock 2013 [PubMed]
  • Multiple (serial) local templates
  • In-house rotamer library (position-specific)
  • Incorporates helix bulges & constrictions [PubMed]
  • Loops modelled by aligned conserved secondary structures, or template chosen by length
  • Models updated continuously with new templates and alignments

Models

  • The models are currently in an expert review phase and the modeling pipeline is being optimized.
